## üé® Premium Design System

### Colors
- **Primary**: Teal (#0d4d52, #14b8a6)
- **Accent**: Sky Blue (#0ea5e9)
- **Gradients**: Multi-stop gradients for depth
- **Overlays**: Dark gradients for text readability

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, large, with letter-spacing
- **Body**: Readable line-height (1.7-1.8)
- **CTAs**: Medium weight, uppercase with spacing

### Spacing
- **Section Padding**: 64px-96px (desktop)
- **Card Padding**: 24px-32px
- **Element Gaps**: 16px-24px

### Shadows & Depth
- **Cards**: Multi-layered shadows
- **Hover States**: Elevated shadows
- **Modals**: Large, soft shadows

### Animations
- **Page Load**: Fade-in-up
- **Scroll**: Parallax effects
- **Hover**: Smooth scale/translate
- **Transitions**: 300-400ms cubic-bezier
